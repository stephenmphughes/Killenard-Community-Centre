FAQ's

Find the answers for the most frequently asked questions below

Electricity/ Lighting for Main Hall

An electricity machine is located at the entrance to the main hall at the bottom of the steps.
Important! The machine accepts €1 & €2 COINS ONLY.

Access to Recycling Bank

The recycling bank is located behind the centre, accessed by the car park. Items accepted are class bottles, alluminium cans and certain clothing items.
Important! Deposit Re-Turn scheme items are not accepted here. Any illegal dumping will be reported.

Key Holders

Currently, we have designated key holders that have access to the center.
Long term bookers can request a key holder form from the commitee.

What Facilities Can I Book?

A sports hall, events stage & 3 studios are available to rent at affordable prices.
